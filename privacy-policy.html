Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

Password Reveal Blocker is an enterprise security extension that hides or disables the password reveal button on approved websites.

Information We Collect

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data Usage

  • Does not collect usernames or passwords.
  • Does not collect browsing history.
  • Does not transmit any data.
  • Does not use analytics.
  • Does not use cookies.

Permissions

The extension only accesses approved websites to hide the password reveal button. It does not read or send any user data.

Third Parties

No information is shared with third parties.
